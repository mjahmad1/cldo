About site

The "Quraner Fariwala" is an institution that illuminates the path of enlightenment. This website is dedicated to the Holy Quran, each verse of which is a pearl from the ocean of knowledge.

Its aim is to make the messages of the Quran accessible and comprehensible to non-Arabic students, enabling them to deeply understand its recitation, meaning, and commentary.

This site stands as a unique establishment for Quranic recitation and research, where the significance and science embedded in each letter of the Quran are revealed.

It represents a noble effort to deliver the true messages of the Quran for the welfare of people worldwide.

The site is managed through MJ-Ahmad's platform, with Md Jafor Ahmad, a Microsoft web developer, at the helm.He created this site purely for the satisfaction of the Creator and spiritual tranquility, not for personal gain.

This site carries a mission of spiritual serenity and is used solely in the service of humanity.

It sends a clear message against those who use this sacred text for commercial means or distort its messages.

Through this site, Md Jafor Ahmad and his team are committed to spreading the authentic words of the Quran to every corner of the world.
